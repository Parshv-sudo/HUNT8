✨ Clue 4: The Three Fairy Gifts

A kind fairy offers you three magical gifts, but one is cursed:

  • 1️⃣ Golden Feather – Grants wisdom, glows softly.
  • 2️⃣ Silver Bell – Calls a protective spirit, rings when danger is near.
  • 3️⃣ Emerald Stone – Said to bring eternal luck, but its glow flickers.

The fairy warns: Any one is safe. The others are either safe or cursed.

Which gift should you accept?
